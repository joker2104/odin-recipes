Description

One kind of sandwich is a burger, which has one or more patties of cooked ground meat, usually beef, inside a sliced bun or bread roll. Other ingredients can also be added to a burger, such as cheese, onion, lettuce, tomato, ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, or other condiments. Burgers are common fast food items that can be delivered online or at restaurants . Some burgers use different kinds of meat, such as chicken, turkey, lamb, or fish, or non-meat fillings, such as tofu, mushrooms, or beans.

Ingredients

  • 1 sliced onion
  • 4 slices cheese slices
  • 1 teaspoon powdered garam masala powder
  • 2 teaspoon refined oil
  • 1/2 gm ginger paste
  • 4 halved burger buns
  • 2 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 1 sliced tomato
  • 2 teaspoon powdered red chilli
  • 2 pinch powdered salt
  • 3 tablespoon breadcrumbs
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 3 tablespoon butter
  • 1 handful chopped coriander leaves
  • 4 leaves lettuce loose-leaf

For The Main Dish

  • 1/2 peeled,sliced cucumber
  • 2 chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup shelled peas
  • 2 mashed,boiled,peeled potato
  • 2 chopped carrot
  • 1/2 cup corn

Steps

  1. Step 1 Cook the carrots, peas and corn in a pressure cooker To prepare the burger patty, cook the carrot, peas and sweet corn in a pressure cooker for 1 whistle or until soft.
  2. Step 2 Mix the spices Mix the cooked vegetables, chopped onions, red chilli powder, lemon juice, garam masala powder, salt and ginger-garlic paste in a large bowl.
  3. Step 3 Add mashed potatoes Add lemon juice and mashed potatoes in the bowl, stir well until well combined. Make small/medium patties from the mixture.
  4. Step 4 Fry the patties Now heat oil in a pan over medium flame. Coat the patties with the breadcrumbs and fry them until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Step 5 Set aside Take them out and set aside.
  6. Step 6 Assemble the burger Take one half of the burger bun. Spread some butter and put the lettuce on it.
  7. Step 7 Add slices onions, cucumber and tomatoes Put the vegetable patty on it. Add slices of onion, tomato, cucumber and cheese. If you want to make it more tasty and healthy use eggless mayonnaise instead of cheese.
  8. Step 8 Burger is ready Put the other half of the burger bun on it. If you like, add some ketchup on it. Use a toothpick to hold it if you want.
  9. Step 9 Serve! Enjoy it with french fries. Please let us know how you liked this recipe, rate it and leave a comment.
